## Ingredients

- 1 bowl crunchy Müsli
- 1 cup Greek yoghurt
- Fruit (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries, bananas)
- 1 tbsp maple syrup

## Preparation

1. Combine everything into a bowl.
2. Eat.
