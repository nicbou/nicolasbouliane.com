title: Lamb kheema 
description: A flavorful mix of minced beef and lamb
date_created: 2025-11-02 
---

## Ingredients

- 3–4 Tbsp vegetable oil  
- 2 medium onions, diced  
- 3 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 inch ginger, grated  
- 500g minced lamb (or lamb and beef)
- 1 large tomato, chopped  
- 1½ tsp salt  
- 1 tsp pepper  
- 1 Tbsp curry powder  
- 1 tsp masala spice  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- 2 Tbsp butter  
- Chopped parsley  
- ½ green chili (save the other half for serving)  
- Pitas, for serving  

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan and brown the onions on low heat.  
2. Add garlic and ginger; cook 2–4 minutes.  
3. Stir in meat, tomato, chili, salt, pepper, and spices; mix well.
4. Cook until the desired texture is reached.  
5. Stir in parsley and butter and cook briefly.  
6. Serve hot with rice or pitas.
