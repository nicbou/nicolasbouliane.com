## Ingredients

- 1 bagel
- 4 slices bacon
- 1 egg, optional
- 2 slices tomatoes
- Lettuce
- Pepper

## Preparation

1. Cook the **bacon**, then the **egg**
2. Slice the **bagel** in half and toast the inside in the greased pan
3. Build a sandwich with the **bacon**, **lettuce**, **tomatoes**, **pepper** and **mayonnaise**, in that order.
