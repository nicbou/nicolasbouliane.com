This is one of my favourite weekday recipes. It's simple, it's quick, it's vegetarian-ish and it's just amazing. Everyone loves it.

## Ingredients

- 250g pasta of your choice
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 2-3 cloves garlic
- 1-2 tablespoons chicken (or vegetable) broth powder
- 300 ml cream
- 2-3 tablespoons parmesan
- A few swigs of dry white wine
- Pepper to taste

You can substitute the parmesan and wine with lemon juice.

## Preparation

1. Start cooking the **pasta** in **salted water**.
2. When you start cooking the pasta, fry the **zucchini** in **olive oil** on medium heat.
3. Fry the **onion** and **garlic** with the zucchini until the onions are soft and translucent.
6. Add the **chicken broth powder** to the onions, then the **cream**, **parmesan** and **white wine**. Reduce temperature to a simmer.
7. Add **pepper** to taste, and use pasta water to control texture.
8. Finish cooking the pasta in the sauce.

### Old version
