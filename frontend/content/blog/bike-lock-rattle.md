Last month, thieves tried to steal my bike before a shopkeeper shooed them off. They mangled my bike lock, so I got a new one.

![Failed bike theft](/images/mangled-bike-lock.jpeg)

This one rattles even louder than the last one. It's all I can hear on my otherwise whisper-quiet bike. I leave my lock at home just to hear myself think!

I shimmed the rattly bits with leftover velcro stickers. I would have used foam tape, but that's all I had at home. The velcro adds a bit of cushioning and dampens the rattle.

![ABUS Gordo rattle fix](/images/bordo-velcro-shim.jpeg)

![ABUS Gordo rattle fix](/images/bordo-velcro-shim-2.jpeg)

There is still a faint rattle *inside* the lock. Perhaps a thicker grease like motorcycle chain oil would help against that, as suggested [here](https://bicycles.stackexchange.com/questions/16156/is-it-normal-that-the-abus-bordo-folding-lock-rattles/100394#100394).
