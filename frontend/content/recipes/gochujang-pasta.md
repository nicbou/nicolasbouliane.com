This is a blend of a few different recipes that use gochujang in pasta sauce. It's closest to the [Mob Kitchen variant](https://old.reddit.com/r/MobKitchen/comments/suju1u/sausage_gochujang_rigatoni/hxa9phc/). My friend makes a different version that leaves out the sausage and uses silken tofu instead of cream.

This recipe is a work in progress, but it's really hard to mess it up.

## Ingredients

- 1 shallot or 1/2 small onion
- 3 cloves garlic
- 200g salciccia
- 100ml vodka (optional)
- 200g cooking cream
- 40g parmesan
- Chili oil (optional)

## Instructions

0. Cook **salciccia** and set aside
1. Sauté **shallots/onions** until golden
2. Add **garlic**, cook for another minute or two
3. Add **gochujang**
4. Add **half the vodka** and let it simmer away.
5. Add **cooking cream**. Simmer down to a nice creamy consistency. 
6. Add **parmesan**, **salciccia** and the rest of the vodka
7. Salt and pepper to taste
8. Use chili oil to add heat as needed.
