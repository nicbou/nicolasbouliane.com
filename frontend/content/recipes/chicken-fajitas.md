## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts, sliced lengthways into thin strips
- 1 red pepper, sliced into strips
- 1 medium red onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- Juice of ½ lime
- Black pepper
- Extra virgin olive oil

**Salsa**

- ½ fresh red chilli (or more), finely chopped
- 8 ripe cherry tomatoes, halved
- 30 g / 1 bunch coriander, chopped with stalks
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 pinch salt
- Black pepper

**To serve**

- Tortillas
- 150 ml yoghurt (or less)
- 50 g cheddar (or more)

## Instructions

1. Put a griddle pan on high heat.
2. Marinate **bell pepper** strips, **onion**, **paprika**, **cumin**, juice of ½ **lime**, **black pepper**, and a drizzle of **olive oil** together.
3. Mix **tomatoes**, **coriander**, **salt**, juice of 1 **lime**, and **black pepper** in a bowl to make the salsa.
4. Cook **chicken** and vegetables on the griddle.
5. Place **tortillas** on top of the pan to soften with steam.
6. Serve with salsa, **yoghurt**, and **cheddar**.
